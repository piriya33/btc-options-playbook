# Strategic Requirements for BTC-Denominated Options Mastery

**Unit of Account:** Bitcoin (Satoshis)  
**Collateral Base:** 1.0 BTC (Initial Experiment)

## 1. Core Objectives

*   **Primary Goal:** Grow the BTC stack count (e.g., 1.0 BTC $\to$ 1.05 BTC per year) regardless of USD price fluctuations.
*   **Secondary Goal:** Generate sustainable "rent" (premium) from market volatility.
*   **Safety Goal:** Zero liquidation risk. The system must survive a 50% "flash crash" or a 100% "God Candle" surge without manual intervention.

## 2. Constraints & Preferences

*   **Execution Profile:** "Traveler-Friendly." No reliance on manual stop-losses or 24/7 monitoring.
*   **Convexity Management:** Mitigation of "Negative Convexity" (where collateral value drops as short put losses rise).
*   **Geometric Opportunity:** The strategy must be structurally positioned to benefit from the Inverse Payout Formula, turning extreme downside volatility into an "automatic dip-buyer" that prints BTC.
*   **Upside Tolerance:** Capping BTC upside is acceptable for premium generation (e.g., selling calls at $120k strike), but the account must remain "Equity Positive" during vertical moves.
